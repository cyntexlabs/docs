tapstate — color system proposal

Fixes for the three R1 caveats: a custom hue (not Tailwind teal-400), a light-mode accent that passes AA, and a lineup test against the neighborhood. Contrast ratios computed, not eyeballed.

1Hue shift: retire teal-400, own 178°

6° cyan-ward from the Tailwind default, 25° from Supabase green, 10° short of Tailwind cyan-400. Contrast on the dark bg improves.

2Two-token accent: dark and light surfaces

Rule: mint is never text on light backgrounds — marks, fills, and strokes only. The dark teal carries links and accents in light mode.

TokenValueUseContrast
--ts-accent#1ED9D2Dark surfaces: links, highlights, mark11.0:1 on #0A0E13 (AAA)
--ts-accent-ink#0B7F79Light surfaces: links, accents, small text4.85:1 on white (AA)
--ts-bg#0A0E13Dark background (unchanged, matches deck v7)—
--ts-panel / --ts-line#10161F / #1D2733Dark cards / borders (unchanged)—

Light-mode demo. Body text in near-black, with an accent link in #0B7F79 that passes AA at normal text size. The mark itself keeps the mint: — graphics may stay bright; text may not. On tinted backgrounds (e.g. #F4F7FA) the ratio drops to 4.5:1, so keep light-mode content surfaces white or darken the accent one step.
